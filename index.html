Our Story

Founded in 2005, Sandy's Plumbing & Heating Co. has been providing top-quality plumbing and heating services to New York residents and businesses for over 18 years. Our founder, Sandy Johnson, started the company with a simple mission: to provide honest, reliable, and professional service at fair prices.

Today, we've grown to a team of skilled technicians, each committed to upholding Sandy's high standards of workmanship and customer service. We stay updated with the latest technologies and techniques to ensure we provide the best solutions for our clients.
