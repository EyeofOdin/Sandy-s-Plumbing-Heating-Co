About Sandy's Plumbing & Heating

Established in 2008, Sandy's Plumbing & Heating Co has been providing quality plumbing and heating services to the New York City community and beyond. With our team of skilled technicians, we deliver reliable solutions to both residential and commercial clients.

We take pride in our commitment to excellence, fair pricing, and customer satisfaction. Our team is licensed, insured, and continuously trained in the latest industry technologies and best practices.
